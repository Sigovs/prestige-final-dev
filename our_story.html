Our Story.

From a North Miami Beach gas station to one of America's leading exotic dealerships — four decades, two generations, one family.

The Beginning

It started with a gas station.

In 1977, Irv David opened Prestige Motorcar Imports on West Dixie Highway — built on excellent service and a personal commitment to every customer. What began as a used European car business would go on to pioneer Florida's exotic car market.

  1. 19771977

    Irv David opens Prestige Motorcar Imports on West Dixie Highway.

  2. 19801980

    Builds Miami's finest exotic used-car inventory.

  3. 19831983

    Buys land on Biscayne Boulevard — a trailer becomes the first sales office.

  4. 19851985

    Maserati franchise acquired; the new showroom rises from the ground.

  5. 19861986

    Grand opening of the new Prestige Imports showroom.

  6. 19881988

    Lamborghini and Lotus franchises join the house.

  7. 19891989

    The last North American Lamborghini Countach is built for Irv David.

  8. 19901990

    Ferruccio Lamborghini signs at the Diablo unveiling in Monte Carlo.

  9. 20112011

    Brett acquires the Pagani franchise and founds the Ride2Revive Foundation.

  10. 20122012

    The AU79 gold Aventador debuts; the Sharpie Lamborghini wins a Picasso Award.

  11. 20142014

    Brett races the Lamborghini Super Trofeo World Final; a Veneno — #2 of 3 — is delivered.

  12. 20152015

    Horacio Pagani presents Project Vulcan; the Audi store sells in a record deal.

  13. 20172017

    Pagani Newport Beach opens; the 40th-anniversary Showroom 2.0 debuts.

  14. 20202020

    The new Lamborghini Miami showroom is completed.

The Next Generation

A nineteen-year-old takes the wheel.

After Irv's passing in 2007, his son Brett — just nineteen — stepped in as CEO. He expanded the house with Pagani, Karma, and a state-of-the-art Lamborghini Miami.

Progressive thinking, the same family standard: the highest level of service and product, carried into a new era.

Giving Back

Ride2Revive.

Founded in 2011, Ride2Revive gives children facing life-threatening illnesses the thrill of a lifetime behind the world's most extraordinary cars — in partnership with Make-A-Wish, Joe DiMaggio Children's Hospital, Nicklaus Children's, and more.
