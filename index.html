The Prestige Difference

A Different Kind of Dealership. Since 1977.

A second-generation family business, Prestige Imports has served South Florida's luxury automotive community since 1977 — and earned a reputation as one of America's premier exotic retailers. Five premium franchises — Lamborghini, Pagani, Lotus, Karma, and Czinger — sit alongside a curated inventory of pre-owned exotics including Ferrari, Bentley, Mercedes, Porsche, Rolls-Royce, and Bugatti.

We don't just sell cars. We provide lifestyles — from acquisition and nationwide delivery through long-term ownership support. For clients who also prefer the water, we offer VanDutch luxury marine alongside our automotive portfolio.
