About This Vehicle

Unleash the ultimate track-bred supercar.

Prestige Imports presents an extraordinary opportunity to own the pinnacle of Lamborghini engineering: a 2022 Lamborghini Huracán STO with just 5,737 miles. Stock #CD2577, VIN ZHWUA6ZX6NLA19976.

The STO — Super Trofeo Omologata — represents Lamborghini's most uncompromising road-legal track weapon, and this example is finished in the breathtaking Atlantis Blue with striking contrast accents. The sophisticated Tan interior creates a purposeful cockpit that puts you at the center of pure automotive passion.

Performance Perfection

At the heart of this beast lies a naturally-aspirated 5.2-liter V10 engine producing earth-shattering power, mated to Lamborghini's lightning-fast 7-speed dual-clutch transmission with sequential shift control. The rear-wheel-drive configuration, combined with a mechanical limited-slip differential and driver-selectable modes, delivers an unfiltered driving experience that few supercars can match.

Track-Focused Engineering

This STO features magnetic fluid-filled shock absorbers, sport-tuned adaptive suspension, and massive 4-wheel ceramic disc brakes that provide fade-free stopping power lap after lap. The aggressive aerodynamics include a prominent wing spoiler, while the lightweight composite and aluminum panels keep weight to a minimum. Forged wheels — 20×8.5" front, 20×11" rear — are wrapped in Bridgestone Potenza Sport tires for maximum grip.

Technology & Luxury

Despite its track focus, the STO doesn't compromise on technology. The 8.4-inch Lamborghini touchscreen infotainment system features 3D navigation with real-time traffic, Apple CarPlay integration, Bluetooth connectivity, and DAB radio. The racing seats are trimmed in premium Sportivo Alcantara with a full Alcantara headliner and carbon-fiber accents throughout. Additional conveniences include dual-zone automatic climate control, front and rear parking sensors, a backup camera, and wireless phone connectivity.

Verified Quality

This Huracán STO comes with an AutoCheck Clean report, providing complete peace of mind. With ultra-low mileage and meticulous care, this is your chance to experience Lamborghini's most extreme creation.
